Description
Submerge yourself in the tranquil underwater world of 17 Patterns Jellyfish collection. Working in collaboration with Athens-born illustrator, artist and textile designer, Christina K, 17 Patterns delivers a beautifully-illustrated pattern of marine life with a delicate narrative. Inspired by Christinas hedonistic love of visual sensations, this aquatic paradise features electric jellyfish with trailing tentacles submerged in an oceanic current of coral reefs and sea anemones. These natural wonders of the world are available in four soothing sceneries (ivory, grey, navy, and black) which are stunningly brought to life with vibrant flourishes of colours such as electric blue, pink, peach and green on high-quality non-woven paper stock. This collection introduces a modern yet traditional backdrop to any interior.
